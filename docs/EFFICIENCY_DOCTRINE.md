# Efficiency doctrine — computer-native neurological AI

**Part of the living thesis** ([THESIS.md](THESIS.md)).

---

## Claim

A neurological **mechanism** (genetic ion-channel programs, FSOT dynamics, E/I microcircuits, multi-region loops) can power artificial intelligence **without** matching the human brain in:

1. **Neuron census** (~10¹¹ cells), or  
2. **Vegetative / interoceptive load** (immune, gut, endocrine, full musculoskeletal plant, continuous autonomic regulation).

Digital substrates compute **faster** than wetware milliseconds. Therefore **functional equivalence** is defined by **structure + measured gates + real-data envelopes**, not by cloning human parameter counts.
